Cookie Clicker Ultimate

I put a full idle game inside the inventory screen. Cookie factories, a research tree, prestige runs. It started because I was bored waiting on furnaces and farms. It runs on its own tick budget so it doesn't affect the rest of the game.

AudioBridge

Shows what you're currently listening to on your HUD. Works with Spotify or whatever's playing system-wide. No setup, just install it and it works. I got tired of alt-tabbing to check the song name.

Better Chunk Borders

The default chunk border visualizer looks bad and causes lag spikes. This one has customizable colors, cleaner grid modes, and has been properly profiled so it stays out of your frame budget. Mostly useful for technical players doing slime chunk or farm layout work.

Background

How I work.

I've been playing since alpha. At some point "playing" turned into "wait, why does the renderer make this decision" and that was pretty much it for me.

Most of my mods come from something I actually wanted. Cookie Clicker Ultimate started because I was going AFK on farms constantly. Better Chunk Borders started because the default one was ugly. That's usually how it goes.

I care a lot about performance. If something I ship costs you frames, I'll keep working on it. It's not really a philosophy, it just bothers me when mods are sloppy about that.
